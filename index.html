Electrical Engineer

About

Genti is an electrical engineer based in New England with a proven track record of designing, building, and shipping hardware from concept through production. His experience covers the full product lifecycle: schematic capture, circuit simulation, PCB layout, EMC compliance, prototype bring-up, test development, and manufacturing support. He has led engineering teams, co-founded an electronics company, and delivered products across telecommunications, medical devices, enterprise computing, and broadcast control. He brings both the hands-on bench skills and the systems-level thinking to solve hard problems and get products out the door.
